#NODES

-Ther are Sending and Receving data this Nodes can be End Nodes(End Devices)
    and Intermediary Nodes(Intermediatry Devies)

1.END NODES
  -End Nodes are the Deives that is going to the Starting Point of Communicastion
       or the end Point to the Communication.
 Ex:-Computers,Phones,Tablets

2.INTERMEDIARY DEIVES
  -If two End Device want to Communicat Each other then he need Intermediary Devices.
 EX:-Switch,Bridges,Hub,Routers,Repeaters
